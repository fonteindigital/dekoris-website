About Us

Who We Are

Dekoris is a proudly Namibian, 100% female-owned interiors company specialising in blinds, curtains, wall panelling, décor, and soft finishes for residential, commercial, and institutional spaces.

With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we offer tailored window treatment solutions to enhance the aesthetic and functional aspects of any environment.

We elevate both homes and businesses through refined craftsmanship and tailored design.

Our Mission

At Dekoris, we are committed to excellence in design and craftsmanship, striving to exceed the expectations of our clients with innovative solutions and unparalleled service. Our mission is to create inspiring spaces that enrich lives and foster lasting connections.

We believe that a living space is a reflection of the individual — speaking to their hopes, beliefs, and unique lifestyle. That's why every solution we offer is tailored to bring beauty, comfort, and functionality to your environment.
