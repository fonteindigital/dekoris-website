Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, services, and process.

General

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Windhoek and serve clients across Namibia. For projects outside Windhoek, please contact us to discuss logistics and availability.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes. We offer complimentary in-home or on-site consultations where we assess your space, discuss your preferences, and take professional measurements.

Products

What types of blinds and curtains do you offer?

We offer a wide range including Roman blinds, Venetian blinds, Honeycomb blinds (25mm and 38mm), Zebra blinds, Roller blinds, and custom curtains. All are available in a variety of colours and fabrics.

Do you offer motorised blinds?

Yes. We offer both manual and motorised options for most of our blind and curtain products. Motorised solutions are ideal for hard-to-reach windows or for added convenience.

Can I see samples before ordering?

Absolutely. During your consultation, we bring fabric and material samples so you can see and feel the options in your own space before making a decision.

Pricing & Orders

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on the product type, window dimensions, fabric selection, and whether you choose manual or motorised operation. We provide a detailed quote after your consultation and measurement.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a deposit is required to confirm your order and begin manufacturing. The balance is due upon completion of installation. Full details are provided with your quote.

How long does delivery and installation take?

Turnaround times vary depending on the product and order size, but most orders are manufactured and installed within 2–3 weeks of confirmation.

Installation & Aftercare

Do you handle the full installation?

Yes. Our team handles everything from measurement to final installation. You don't need to arrange anything separately.

What if something goes wrong after installation?

We stand behind our work. If you experience any issues after installation, contact us and we'll arrange a service visit to resolve it.

Do you work with businesses and institutions?

Yes. We provide commercial installations for offices, healthcare facilities, hospitality venues, and other institutional spaces. We can handle large-scale projects with multiple windows and rooms.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
